嶺南建築與世界主義

Lingnan Architecture and Cosmopolitanism

時間

2023.03.18（六）

10:00 EST／16:00 英國／22:00 台灣

對談人
李士橋、彭長歆、馮江、羅坤、郭偉杰、伍江、童明
主持人
張琴、張晉維

Time

Saturday, March 18th, 2022

10:00 EST / 16:00 GMT / 22:00 CST

Guest speakers
Li Shiqiao, Peng Changxin, Feng Jiang, Cole Roskam, Jeffrey W. Cody, Wu Jiang, Tong Ming
Moderator
Moderators: Zhang Qin, Chang Chin-Wei

由於嶺南天然獨特的地理氣候環境、歷史文化脈絡以及與港澳及海外相對緊密的經濟貿易聯繫，嶺南建築成為中國近現代建築歷史中的重要組成部分。1970–1980 年代，隨著外交關係改善、對外經貿發展，嶺南地區的國際賓館等旅居空間如雨後春筍般湧現，成為「改革開放的重要載體形式」。嶺南建築師對亞熱帶氣候適應性、地域性傳統庭園、現代主義風格等不同方向的設計探索，也體現在這一重要建築類型上。本次研討會以此為切入點，梳理旅居空間建築類型、建築師創作理念的變化發展脈絡，構建起與背後社會經濟文化之間的聯繫，從全新的角度探討嶺南建築與世界主義的深層關聯。


Due to Lingnan’s unique geography, climate, history and culture, and close economic relation with the outer world, Lingnan architecture is an important part of the history of Chinese modern architecture. From the 1970s to the 1980s, with China’s turn toward reform and opening, international hotels in the Lingnan area became an important booming typology, with different design explorations such as adapting to subtropical climate, introducing traditional Lingnan gardens, as well as Modernism. This symposium takes international hotels in the Lingnan Region as an entry point to discuss its typology history and design concept development, and brings important insights into their relationship to the political, economic, and diplomatic context during the transitional period, and links Lingnan architecture and Cosmopolitanism from a new perspective.
